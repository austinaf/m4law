How Important is the Right Law Firm?

Critically Important.

Trusting the right law firm to represent you or your business is essential. That firm should understand you, your company, and offer creative solutions to your commercial and legal challenges.

At McCorriston Miller Mukai MacKinnon LLP (M4), we always maintain a keen eye on the big picture.

A Firm That Listens to You

We understand that the most valuable legal advice comes after walking in our client's shoes. Our clients demand real world advice, not law school answers.

We meet our client's demands by providing the same type of advice we would want: solution-oriented, practical, and timely.

We also offer an experienced group of attorneys who have represented both plaintiffs and defendants in a wide range of constitutional matters. Our attorneys handle a variety of constitutional claims including equal protection, eminent domain, and due process issues.
